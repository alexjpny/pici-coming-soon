Frequently Asked Questions

A collection of frequently asked questions we receive as well as questions commonly asked around different restaurants and eateries.
Can I make a reservation at Pici?
Of course, we offer reservations for party’s of 8 people. If you would like to enquire about a larger booking, please get in contact and we’ll try to accommodate where possible.
do you cater for dietary requirements or allergies?
we will try to accommodate your needs where possible. we don't currently accommodate for coeliacs as we work in a very floury environment and can't avoid cross-contamination.
is booking necessary or can we walk in?
we do have walk-in spots available but these are first-come first-served and are very limited. please book to ensure your spot.
do you sell gift vouchers?
yes we do! please follow this link here to see our offerings.
is there parking nearby?
there are quite a few parking options not too far walk from our restaurant.
are you child friendly?
yes we are up until 9pm due to standard alcohol licensing.
what food is served at pici?
fresh pasta, pizzettes, small plates, wood fire wine, spritz
